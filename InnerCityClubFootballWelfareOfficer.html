My Responsibility

To be clear about the clubs responsibilities when running activities for children and young people. This involves:

  • Ensuring these responsibilities are well understood by others
  • Promoting The FA’s Respect Programme and helping to develop best practice processes

It is my responsibilty to help club personnel understand what their ‘duty of care’ towards children and young people actually means and entails on a day-to-day basis. In order to carry out my responsibilities I will look at the following but not limited to:

  • To put in place a safeguarding children policy, anti-bullying policy and equality policy
  • To understand how to refer a concern about the welfare of a child
  • Communicate with parents/spectators and get them to sign up to the Respect codes
  • Communitcate with parents and new players by getting involved with running ‘start of season’ welcome sessions for members
  • Communicate with coaches and managers about the importance of being consistent role models for their players
  • To encourage coaches, team managers, first aiders/medics to complete The FA’s Safeguarding Children Workshop
  • To encourage the committee to make use of the Respect Programmes designated spectator area at all games
  • To monitor compliance with FA CRB checks through The FA CRB Unit for those who require one using The FA Safeguarding Online System
